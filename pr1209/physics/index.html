Physics

This section documents the physics conventions that EOS uses implicitly in the predictions of flavour physics observables. It collects the choices of units, the effective field theories and their operator bases, the definitions of the hadronic matrix elements of local operators together with their parametrisations, and the observables that EOS predicts for semileptonic decays.
